About Me

Mother to two young children, lifelong creative and learner. Carina is an actor, writer, editor, vocalist, songwriter, educator and healing practitioner. A multi-disciplinary medicine woman. 22 years experience educating young people in a variety of roles; group and 1:1 educator/tutor. 25 years of live performance, including composition, writing and collaboration for theater, music, film, tv and dance. Co-founder of the award‑winning Masamama Bakery in Arequipa, Perú.
